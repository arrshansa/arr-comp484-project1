Why is Interstellar my favorite movie?

In my opinion, Interstellar is not just a movie, it's an emotional journey that explores the mysteries of the universe and the power of an emotion that some of us overlook, love. The movies's stunning visuals, compelling storytelling, and profound themes left me speechless and astounded from the very first time I watched the movie. And yes, I have watched it multiple times, probably more than a 100 times over the past 11 years. I was 10 years old when the movie came out and I still remember walking out of the theater with my family being completely spellbound by what I had just watched. I would always research more about the movie and dug up every single detail about how it was made.

Themes of Interstellar

Time

Lesson of time and the price we pay for every second spent away from those we love.

Love

The only force that transcends dimensions of time and space.

Exploration

Humanity's innate drive to explore the unknown, to push beyond our limits and reach for the stars.

Survival

The desperate fight for humanity's survival in the face of environmental collapse and planetary extinction.
